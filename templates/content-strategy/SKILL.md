### 4. Content Sequencing
For any major release (report, event, product launch), plan a sequenced rollout:

1. **Teaser** (2-3 weeks before) — social posts, email preview, internal announcement
2. **Launch** (release day) — blog post, press release, email blast, social campaign
3. **Deep dives** (1-2 weeks after) — webinar, podcast, case studies, Q&A
4. **Evergreen** (ongoing) — gated download, resource library, sales enablement
