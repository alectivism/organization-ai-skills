## Brief Formats

### Board / Executive Update
**Length:** 1 page
**Structure:**
- Program name + one-line status
- Key milestone since last update
- Data point or finding (if new)
- Next milestone with date
- Decision needed (if any)

### Member / Client Newsletter Section
**Length:** 200-300 words
**Structure:**
- Headline finding
- Why it matters for the reader's business
- How to get involved or access the full research
- CTA

### Internal Team Update
**Length:** Flexible
**Structure:**
- Status (on track / at risk / completed)
- Progress since last update
- Key findings or deliverables
- Blockers or needs
- Next steps with owners and dates

### Public-Facing Summary
**Length:** 500-800 words
**Structure:**
- Context: why this research exists and who it serves
- Methodology (brief — accessible to non-specialists)
- Top findings with data
- Implications for the target audience
- About [YOUR ORGANIZATION] (boilerplate)
