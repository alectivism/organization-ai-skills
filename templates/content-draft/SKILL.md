### Writing Principles
- **Lead with insight, not introduction** — skip "[In today's rapidly evolving landscape...]"
- **Data-first claims** — back assertions with [YOUR ORGANIZATION]'s research where possible
- **Active voice** throughout
- **Specific over vague** — "[actual numbers]" not "[vague superlatives]"

- **Subheads every 2-3 paragraphs** for scannability
